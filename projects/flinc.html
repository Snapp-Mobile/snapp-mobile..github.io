Flinc

Flinc is the leading ridesharing service in Germany. Backed by BMW and Deutsche Bahn, Flinc has a growing network of drivers and passengers.


The Flinc service automatically matches passengers to the best drivers based on intelligent routing with a goal of getting you door-to-door with the help of a friendly driver.


For drivers, Flinc handles pricing proposals so as to take away awkward negotiation between driver and passenger.


The Project

Our connection with Flinc came through a member of their Android team, with whom we worked on a previous project. Tight deadlines and a critical release meant they needed help to get through their backlog.


We were able to drop straight into their mobile development team and get to work building key features for the upcoming release.


In contrast to projects where we represent the entire mobile development team, this engagement is a good example of how we also like to come in and be part of an existing team to lend a hand where needed.
