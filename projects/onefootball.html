The Project

We were asked by the wonder Onefootball team to help bring their already popular Android app into line with Google's user interface guidelines. This included defining a truly responsive app design that spans multiple screen devices from small phones to large tablets.


In the middle of the project, Google release the Material Design standards. Naturally we took this in our stride and incorporated this new design language.


The app became one of the early examples of a Material Design implementation and has since made the Google Play Editor's Choice list three years in a row.
