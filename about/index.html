About Snapp Mobile

Find out why companies hire us.
What we do
iOS & Android

Companies hire us to build excellent iOS and Android apps. Beyond that, we are often asked to help with broader architecture challenges that accompany mobile systems.

Our Approach
Software Engineering

Everyone at Snapp is a software engineer with many years of experience. If you want great code and sensible decisions, we can help. We don't do fancy presentations or slick pitches - just great software development.

Success criteria
Practical & Pragmatic

We care about usability, code & architecture quality, transparency and what happens post-project. Common pitfalls we work hard to avoid include over engineering, feature bloat and app store release inertia.
